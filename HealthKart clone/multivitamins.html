    In case you miss the dose, take the medicine immediately as soon as you remember, but if it is almost the time to take your next dose, then skip the missed dose. Avoid taking 2 doses at one time.

    What Happens if I Overdose?

    If you overdose on fat soluble vitamins like vitamins A, D, E and K can cause life threatening side effects but that doesn’t mean you overdose on water soluble vitamins, it's a big no. Overdose symptoms may include stomach pain, diarrhea, loss of appetite, constipation, vomiting, weight loss, back pain, hair loss and much more.

    It completely depends on how medications work in your body and multivitamins interact with certain medications like antibiotics, antacid, water pill, blood pressure or heart medications.     Get medical help immediately if you see signs of allergic reaction like difficulty in breathing, hives, swelling of the lips, face, tongue and throat. Common side effects of multivitamins are upset stomach, headache and unpleasant taste in the mouth.
